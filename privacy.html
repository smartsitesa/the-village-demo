Privacy Policy

How The Village by Annè handles website visits, enquiries and contact details.

Last updated: 15 May 2026

Your privacy matters

The Village by Annè uses this website to share information, receive enquiries and help visitors contact us through phone, email and WhatsApp. We only ask for details needed to respond to your enquiry.

Information we may receive

  • Your name, phone number, email address and message if you choose to contact us.
  • Basic enquiry details such as dates, number of guests, services requested or product interest.
  • Website analytics information such as pages viewed, device type and general traffic statistics.

How we use information

  • To reply to enquiries and booking/product questions.
  • To improve the website and understand which pages are useful to visitors.
  • To communicate through the contact method you choose, including WhatsApp, phone or email.

Analytics and third-party services

This website uses Google Analytics to understand site performance and visitor activity in aggregate. The site also links to WhatsApp, Google Maps and Facebook. When you open those services, their own privacy policies apply.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Details may only be shared where needed to respond to your enquiry, operate the website, comply with law, or use trusted service providers such as analytics, hosting and communication platforms.

Your choices

You can choose not to provide contact details. You may also ask us to update or delete enquiry details we hold, where legally and practically possible.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact The Village by Annè at info@thevillagebyanne.co.za or WhatsApp 083 446 8070.
